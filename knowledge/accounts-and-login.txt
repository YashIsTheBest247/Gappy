Accounts, Login & SSO
=====================

Password reset
- Use "Forgot password" on the login page. The reset link is valid for 60 minutes.
- If the email never arrives, check spam, then confirm the address is the one invited to the workspace.

Single Sign-On (SSO)
- SSO (SAML and Google Workspace) is available on the Scale plan.
- Once SSO is enforced, password login is disabled for all members except the workspace owner,
  who keeps a break-glass password login.
- To set up SSO: Settings > Security > SSO, upload your IdP metadata, then test with one account
  before enforcing for everyone.

Two-factor authentication (2FA)
- 2FA via authenticator app is available on all plans and can be required workspace-wide on Growth and above.
- Lost 2FA device: the workspace owner can reset 2FA for a member under Settings > Members.

Roles and access
- Roles are Owner, Admin, Member, and Viewer. Only Owner and Admin can change billing or security settings.
- Removing a member immediately revokes their sessions.

Account deletion
- A workspace owner can delete the workspace under Settings > Danger Zone. Deletion is permanent
  after a 7-day grace period, during which it can be restored by contacting support.
