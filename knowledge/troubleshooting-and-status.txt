Troubleshooting & Status
========================

Service status
- Live status and incident history are at status.example.com. Subscribe there for incident emails.
- During a confirmed incident, the in-app banner links to the status page; we post updates every 30 minutes.

Dashboards not loading / stale data
- First try a hard refresh and a different network. Most "stuck" dashboards are a cached browser session.
- Data sync runs every 15 minutes by default. If a connected source is behind, check
  Settings > Sources for the last sync time and any error on that source.
- A red dot on a source means the connection's credentials expired; reconnect from that source's page.

Charts showing wrong numbers
- Confirm the date range and timezone (Settings > Workspace > Timezone). Numbers are computed in the
  workspace timezone, which can differ from your local time.
- Filters are sticky per dashboard. A surprising number is often a filter left applied from earlier.

Reporting a bug
- Include: what you expected, what happened, the dashboard or URL, and the time it occurred (with timezone).
- Screenshots and the browser console log speed up diagnosis a lot.

Performance
- Dashboards with 20+ widgets render slower; splitting into focused dashboards is the recommended fix.
