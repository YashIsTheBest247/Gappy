Billing & Refunds Policy
=======================

Plans and billing cycle
- Plans are Starter, Growth, and Scale. Billing is monthly or annual.
- Annual plans are billed up front and are discounted 20% versus monthly.
- Seats can be added at any time; new seats are prorated for the remainder of the cycle.

Refunds
- We offer a full refund within 14 days of the first payment on a new subscription.
- After 14 days, monthly plans are non-refundable but can be cancelled to stop future charges.
- Annual plans cancelled after 14 days are not refunded for the remaining term, but the plan
  stays active until the end of the paid term.
- Accidental duplicate charges are always refunded in full. Refunds reach the card in 5-10
  business days depending on the bank.

Failed payments
- If a charge fails we retry on days 1, 3, and 5. The account stays active during retries.
- After the third failed retry the account moves to read-only until payment succeeds.

Changing or cancelling
- Plan changes and cancellation are self-serve under Settings > Billing.
- Downgrades take effect at the next renewal; upgrades take effect immediately and are prorated.

Invoices and tax
- Invoices are emailed to the billing contact and available under Settings > Billing > Invoices.
- VAT/GST numbers can be added under Settings > Billing > Company details before an invoice is issued.
